Welcome to Alphabots FTC Team 19639

We are a Bay Area-based high school robotics team passionately learning, competing, and growing through the FIRST® Tech Challenge program. Join us on our journey to engineer the future.

Team Photo

Our Mission

Facilitate members of the organization and the community to learn more about math and science, while expanding their knowledge of careers involving computer programming, business, engineering and robotics. Our goal is also to spread STEM awareness by getting young people interested in these fields to get them to pursue their interests further.

About Us

We are a group of 13 to 15-year-olds with a passion for robotics. We have the drive to compete, trying to learn and also spread the word about computer science, business, and engineering.

Our robotics team members build and program robots to compete at regional competitions, potentially qualifying for the NorCal State tournament and World Championship. Our team consists of 9 students from multiple middle and high schools in the Fremont Unified School District. We have 2 coaches and 2 mentors on the team.

Alphabots is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization (EIN: 81-1543325) based in Fremont, California representing a team participating in the FIRST® Tech Challenge program.

Community Outreach

As part of our wider aim to empower ourselves and others through STEM, we hold various events. Getting young people interested in these fields is very important, not only to our community, but also to our state and country.


FIRST® Tech Challenge Impact

FIRST® Tech Challenge is an out-of-school program for students (grades 7-12) to learn about STEM through hands-on robotics. Participating allows us to expand our knowledge of computer programming, business, and engineering.
