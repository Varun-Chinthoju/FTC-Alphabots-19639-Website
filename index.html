Engineering the Future

We are a Bay Area-based robotics team passionate about STEM, competition, and community.

Our Mission

Our Goal

Facilitate members of the organization and the community to learn more about math and science, while expanding their knowledge of careers involving computer programming, business, engineering and robotics.

Who We Are

We are a group of 13 to 15-year-olds with a passion for robotics. Our robotics team members build and program robots to compete at regional competitions, potentially qualifying for the NorCal State tournament and World Championship. Our team consists of 9 students from multiple middle and high schools in Fremont.

Organization

Alphabots is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization (EIN: 81-1543325) based in Fremont, California representing a team participating in the FIRST® Tech Challenge program.

Community Outreach

Spreading STEM awareness by inspiring young minds.

FIRST® Tech Challenge Impact

FIRST® Tech Challenge is an out-of-school program for students (grades 7-12) to learn about STEM through hands-on robotics. Participating allows us to expand our knowledge of computer programming, business, and engineering.


As part of our wider aim to empower ourselves and others through STEM, we hold various events. Getting young people interested in these fields is very important, not only to our community, but also to our state and country.
